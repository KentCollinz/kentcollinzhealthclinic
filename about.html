Your Health, Our Priority

About KentCollinz Health Clinic

KentCollinz Health Clinic is a modern, patient-centered medical facility dedicated to providing accessible, reliable, and compassionate healthcare to individuals and families within our community. We combine evidence-based medical practices, modern diagnostic tools, and a personalized approach to ensure every patient receives the highest standard of care.

At KentCollinz Health Clinic, your health is more than a service — it is our priority.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, quality, and affordable healthcare that improves the lives of our patients and supports the wellbeing of the community.

Our Vision

To become a trusted health center known for excellence in patient care, medical innovation, and community impact.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with empathy, respect, and dignity.
  • Professionalism: We adhere to the highest medical and ethical standards.
  • Integrity: Honesty, transparency, and confidentiality guide our work.
  • Quality Care: We strive for accurate diagnosis and effective treatment.
  • Community Focus: We promote wellness through education and preventive care.

What We Do

  • General Medical Consultations
  • Laboratory Testing and Diagnostics
  • Specialized Care (General Medicine, Pediatrics, Lab Science & more)
  • Maternal & Child Health Services
  • Vaccinations, Screening & Preventive Healthcare
  • Health Education and Wellness Guidance

Our Approach to Healthcare

We believe that great healthcare goes beyond treating symptoms — it involves understanding the whole person. Our approach includes:

  • Use of modern diagnostic tools
  • Clear communication and education
  • Follow-up support for long-term wellness

Looking Ahead

Our Journey

KentCollinz Health Clinic began as a vision to provide better healthcare access.

2022 – Official Opening

The clinic opened its doors with core services in general medicine and laboratory diagnostics.

2024 – Services Expanded

Added pediatrics, maternal care, and preventive health programs.

2025 – Digital Health Integration

Introduced online consultations, digital records, and WhatsApp support.
