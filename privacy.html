Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

At KentCollinz Health Clinic, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information — Name, phone number, email address, or any information you submit through contact forms or WhatsApp.
  • Health-related Information — Only when you voluntarily submit details for booking consultations or medical inquiries.
  • Technical Information — IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited (via Google Analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide medical consultations and healthcare services
  • To communicate with you regarding appointments and inquiries
  • To improve our website performance and user experience
  • To enhance clinic operations based on analytics data

3. WhatsApp Communication

When you contact us via WhatsApp, your messages are protected under WhatsApp’s end-to-end encryption. However, WhatsApp may collect metadata such as your phone number. Your messages with the clinic are kept private and never shared with third parties.

4. Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to measure traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics may collect:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Device type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser information

This data helps us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve our content and services.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve loading speed and user experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

6. How We Protect Your Information

We protect your data through:

  • Secure servers and encrypted website protocols (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to personal data
  • Strict confidentiality from all clinic staff

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your information is only shared when:

  • Required by law or government authorities
  • Necessary for providing medical services (e.g., medical referrals)

8. Patient Confidentiality

All medical information and patient records are handled in strict compliance with medical ethics and confidentiality guidelines. No medical details are shared without your consent.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The updated version will always be posted on this page.
